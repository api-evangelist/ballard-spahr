President Trump’s May 19, 2026 executive order , Restoring Integrity to America’s Financial System , directs Treasury, FinCEN, the CFPB, and the federal banking agencies to reassess how financial institutions identify and manage risks associated with non-work authorized populations and related cross border financial activity.
